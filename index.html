About Music Studio Services

Justin DiCenzo, owner of Music Studio Services, brings extensive technical expertise from years of professional audio work in film and television production. As a Grammy Award winner for his work on Anderson .Paak's Ventura, and with credits on major productions including Get Out (score technical engineer), The Good Place (music mixer), Arrested Development (music coordinator), and Superstore (additional music composer), Justin has solved complex audio technical challenges on numerous high-profile projects.

This Grammy-winning, film and TV production experience - spanning technical engineering on Academy Award-winning films to music coordination for major network television - means Justin understands audio systems under pressure. When deadlines are tight and equipment absolutely must work, he's been there and solved it at the highest level.

Whether it's troubleshooting driver conflicts, optimizing plugin performance, configuring complex audio routing, or managing technical workflows for Grammy-winning albums and major network television, Justin has dealt with these issues in high-stakes professional environments where failure isn't an option.

Now Justin applies this Grammy and professional film/TV troubleshooting expertise to help musicians and content creators in the Saint Louis area get their home studios working reliably. No more wrestling with technology when you should be making music.
